BESTIARY

Snared Flea

Health: 3 Shovel Hits | Danger Level: 30%

Fleas in the "Snare" variety dangle from overhead surfaces and descend upon detecting nearby movement from a player. Their aim is to affix themselves to the player's head, seeking to hinder their breathing and potentially cause suffocation.

Bunker Spider

Health: 5 Shovel Hits | Danger Level: 20%

They display a high level of aggression and territorial behavior, roaming throughout the facility and strategically weaving webs to ensnare potential prey. If a player comes into contact with one of these webs, the creature becomes alerted and actively pursues the respective player.

Hoarding Bug

Health: 2 Shovel Hits | Danger Level: 20%

They are commonly referred to as "Loot Bugs" due to their tendency to collect scrap. Provide caution when stealing their loot.

Bracken

Health: 5 Shovel Hits | Danger Level: 70%

Silently, they track players with the intent of swiftly breaking their necks and carrying off their bodies. Brief eye contact deters them, but extended gaze provokes their aggression.

Thumper

Health: 4 Shovel Hits | Danger Level: 70%

Although lacking in hearing abilities, they possess remarkable eyesight. They exhibit exceptional speed in movement but encounter difficulty navigating around corners.

Hygrodere

Health: Immune | Danger Level: 0%

Referred to as Blobs, Slimes, or Ooze, these entities are sluggish and shapeless. They leisurely traverse the facility, causing harm to players ensnared within their amorphous forms.

Ghost Girl

Health: Immune | Danger Level: Unknown

She targets one player at a time, remaining visible solely to that individual. Initially appearing from a distance, she gradually approaches and ultimately eliminates the chosen player.

Spore Lizard

Health: Immune | Danger Level: 5%

Primarily tranquil, they pose a threat to players only when provoked or actively pursued.

Nutcracker

Health: 5 Shovel Hits | Danger Level: 90%

During pauses to survey their surroundings, their heads elevate, unveiling a solitary eye on a stalk. Exceptionally responsive to movement, they utilize a shotgun to attack players within their range and resort to kicking those who come too close.

Coil Head

Health: Immune | Danger Level: 80%

Despite their swiftness and lethality, they can only engage in movement and attacks when not under direct observation by employees.

Jester

Health: Immune | Danger Level: 95%

Presenting themselves as petite jack-in-the-boxes with legs initially, they may come across as somewhat cute due to their small stature. Nonetheless, exercise caution, as these creatures are ticking time bombs, and only one jester can emerge at a time.

Masked

Health: 4 Shovel Hits | Danger Level: Unknown

These masks are often discovered in deserted locations, initially appearing as seemingly harmless pieces of scrap. However, employees who hold the masks to their faces for an extended duration will don the mask and succumb to its control, transforming into a Masked. The Masked individuals then pursue other employees, capturing and converting them into more Masked entities.
